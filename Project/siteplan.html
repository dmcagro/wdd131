Dmc Agro Farm Products & Greenhouses

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Dmc Agro Farm Products & Greenhouses

This name reflects the website's focus on greenhouse production, farm products, and agricultural input supplies. It represents fresh harvests, sustainable farming, and modern agriculture.

Logo Idea: A green leaf inside a greenhouse structure.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about greenhouse farming, farm products, and agricultural input supplies. Visitors can learn about greenhouse technology, crop production, available farm inputs, and ways to improve farm productivity. The site will also showcase products and services offered to farmers.

Scenarios

  • What farm inputs are available for greenhouse vegetable production?
  • How can I set up a greenhouse to increase crop yield throughout the year?
  • What types of farm products can I purchase from GreenHarvest?
  • How do I contact the company for agricultural consultation or supplies?
